Who we are

astrobagg is an Instagram account publishing daily astrology content. Contact: eneskarsli009@gmail.com

What we collect

Only what you send us voluntarily by direct message, and only to produce your personal reading: date of birth, time of birth (optional), city of birth, your Instagram username and message content.

You are not required to provide any of this. Without it you simply receive the public general horoscopes.

Why we process it

Solely to compute your birth chart (Swiss Ephemeris) and write your daily reading. The computed planetary positions are sent to a language model to draft the text; identifying details such as your name or username are not included in that request.

What we never do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use it for ad targeting.
  • We do not share it with third parties for marketing.
  • We do not give medical, financial or legal advice. Content is for entertainment and personal insight.

Retention and deletion

Your birth details are kept so we can continue sending your daily reading. Message us "delete my data" on Instagram, or email eneskarsli009@gmail.com. We erase it within 30 days.

Your rights
